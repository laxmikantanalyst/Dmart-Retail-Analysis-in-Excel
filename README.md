# 🛒 D-Mart Retail Sales Dashboard (Excel)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project is an interactive **D-Mart Retail Sales Dashboard** built entirely in **Microsoft Excel**. The dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of sales performance, profit analysis, product performance, regional performance, and customer payment behavior.

The purpose of this project is to transform raw retail sales data into meaningful business insights using Excel's advanced features such as Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, Slicers, KPIs, and Dashboard Design techniques.

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze overall sales and profit performance.
- Track monthly sales trends.
- Compare sales across product categories.
- Analyze customer payment preferences.
- Identify top-performing products.
- Compare profit generated by different regions.
- Build an interactive dashboard for business decision-making.

## 📈 Key Business Insights

### Sales Performance
- Total Sales exceeded **₹126 Million**.
- Consistent sales observed throughout the year.
- December recorded one of the highest sales values.

### Category Analysis
- Sales contribution is distributed relatively evenly across categories.
- Household and Grocery categories contribute significantly to overall sales.

### Payment Analysis
- UPI and Cash are among the most preferred payment methods.
- Digital payments contribute a major share of transactions.

### Regional Analysis
- South region generated the highest profit.
- East region generated comparatively lower profit.

### Product Analysis
Top-selling products include:
- Chips
- Detergent
- Sugar
- Tissue Paper
- Tea
